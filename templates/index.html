In the time when the world has come on the internet for education full time, it's equally important for these platforms to be strongly backed up by technology. One of the major loopholes is plagiarism and copying in online exams. To tackle this issue, we bring to you 'Check Mate'. With our tool, teachers and professors can get all the pdf documents compared and checked for plagiarism.The web app includes feature like uploading files in pdf formats, comparing a document with the rest of the submissions and gives a comparison score, depicting percentage of content copied. Depending on a threshold comparison score, an automated email will generated and sent to the respective person's email id with the similarity scores.
